Appliances

  • Residents who have city waste collection service can request a pickup of appliances by calling LexCall at 311 or (859) 425-2255.
  • There is no charge for picking up these appliances. Appliances containing Freon will be accepted, but appliances containing oil are not. Pickups are normally made within five business days of your request. This service is for households only.

Before pick up

  • Remove doors from refrigerators and freezers
  • Place appliance at the curb for collection

Appliance drop-off

  • All Fayette County residents and apartment complexes can recycle appliances at no cost by dropping them off to Environmental Recycling Inc.
  • 3899 Winchester Road
  • Monday–Friday
  • 7 a.m.–3 p.m.
